Introduction

Your future employer surely wants to know who he's hiring as a person and if that person fits in his or her team. So it's good practise to give some kind of introduction on what you are all about. There are a few general topics to describe your personality, these include hobbies, language, religion, heritage, culture, philosophy, music, pets, sports.

Examples

Here's an example on how to show your hobbies or interests. Provide some tangibility if you can.

Motivation

Make sure your passion for your work comes across. What drives you? People don't want a grumpy co-worker. When people know you love what you do, people will like working with you.


Social dynamics

Give some insight on how you function within a group: are you the leading type, maybe a perfectionist or do you prefer the background? What are you bringing to the team? What traits define you as a co-worker? How do you handle stress or deadlines?
