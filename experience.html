Introduction

Experience is important when looking for a job. It tells you that you not only have what it takes to do the work, it also means you are able to carry it out in a professional environment. Can you perform under pressure? Can you solve problems before the deadline? Are you a teamplayer? Can people count on you?

The first experience is always the hardest. Companies want experienced people so landing the first job will be tough. Our example student has made their way into an internship for a small web development company.

Internship

Internship

It's a decent way to get some experience. Downside is you won't be paid in full and you probably already did some internships during school as well, but for some reason they dont often count for actual experience since its part of the school program.

Resume

Resume

It is important to have a clear resume. Google has some templates on resume lay-out. For IT however, it may be nice to not only include education and experience, but also some way to show your proficiency in the tools you use.
